Assignment 13C.01.02 Working with colors and animations

In this assignment, I got to work with animations and more specifically I got to work with API animations and other general JavaScript animations.

A few animations

In this assignment, I've decided to put in a few different sorts of animation as seen on the right. Most of the animations trigger on load and the rest on scroll (or inView). I decided to keep the animations simple, to not create too much chaos on the site.

Darkmode theme

As we also had to implement a darkmode theme, I decided to do so. I choose the colors from the original color pallette and made them darker pretty much. My lightmode primary color became the darkmodes accent color and the other way around.

Motivation for the Final solution

For this assignment I went with a simple color scheme. It's pretty much purple all around in different gradients and on top of that there is a bit of black and light blue. I chose the dark purple to be the accent color, due to everything being light, so the dark from that purple will create focus where it's needed.

As already explained the darkmode is mostly the same colors as the lightmode, but the colors are used differently to create the same sort of contrast there is in the lightmode theme.
